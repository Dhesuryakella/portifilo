Who I Am

I'm Dhesurya Kella, a final-year B.Tech student in Electronics and Communication Engineering at MVGR College of Engineering, Vizianagaram. My journey in engineering has been driven by a passion for creating intelligent systems that bridge the gap between hardware and software.

With hands-on experience in embedded firmware development, computer vision, and machine learning, I specialize in building real-time systems that solve practical problems. From developing ESP32-based communication protocols to creating AI-powered crowd safety systems, I thrive on challenges that push the boundaries of what's possible.

My work has been recognized through publications at IEEE conferences, and I've had the privilege of conducting research at prestigious institutions like IIT Tirupati and NITK Surathkal. I'm particularly interested in safety-critical systems, aerospace applications, and the intersection of embedded systems with artificial intelligence.
